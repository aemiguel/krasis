The fundamental intuition behind the Binary Chop is to eliminate half of the remaining search space with every comparison.

Imagine looking up a word in a physical dictionary. You do not start at page 1 and read every word. Instead, you open the book roughly in the middle.
*   If the word you want comes **alphabetically before** the current page, you ignore the second half of the book.
*   If it comes **after**, you ignore the first half.
*   You repeat this process on the remaining half until you find the word.
